Five Interesting Football Facts

Facts

  • Fact 1: The FIFA World Cup is the most-watched sporting event in the world, with over 3.5 billion viewers during the tournament.
  • Fact 2: The first official football match was played in 1863 in England. It was between two teams from London, and the rules were formalized in that same year.
  • Fact 3: Brazil has won the most World Cup titles, with a total of five championships (1958, 1962, 1970, 1994, 2002).
  • Fact 4: The fastest goal ever scored in a World Cup match was by Hakan Şükür of Turkey, who scored just 11 seconds into the game against South Korea in 2002.
  • Fact 5: The largest football stadium in the world by capacity is the Rungrado 1st of May Stadium in Pyongyang, North Korea, which can hold up to 114,000 spectators.
